K-TIRADS 2021 Guidelines:
K-TIRADS 1: No thyroid nodule
K-TIRADS 2: Benign (malignancy risk < 3%): iso-/hyperechoic spongiform nodule, partially cystic with echogenic foci and comet tail artifacts, pure cyst [Biopsy: Not indicated]
K-TIRADS 3: Low suspicion (malignancy risk 3-10%): isoechoic or partially cystic nodules without suspicious US features. [Biopsy: > 20 mm]
K-TIRADS 4: Intermediate suspicion (malignancy risk 10-40%): solid hypoechoic nodule without suspicious US feature / isoechoic or partially cystic nodules with suspicious US features. Entirely calcified nodule. [Biopsy: > 10-15 mm]
K-TIRADS 5: High suspicion (malignancy risk > 60%): solid hypoechoic nodule with suspicious US features. [Biopsy: > 10 mm]
